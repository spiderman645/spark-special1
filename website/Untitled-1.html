About the Course

This course is designed to empower individuals with Down syndrome by teaching essential life skills, fostering creativity, and promoting independence through online learning.

Course Highlights

  • 3-year program with progressive learning modules.
  • Interactive and accessible content.
  • Support for caregivers and families.

Curriculum Overview

Year 1: Building Foundations

Focus on basic life skills, communication, and introductory concepts.

Year 2: Expanding Skills

Build on foundational skills and introduce more advanced concepts.

Year 3: Independence and Self-Expression

Develop greater independence, self-expression, and advanced skills.
